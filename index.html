About me

The nurse who refused
to stay in one lane.

I am a Registered Nurse and Public Health professional by training — and a CAD designer, video producer, AI specialist, and electrical design enthusiast by passion. People are often surprised by that combination. I stopped being surprised a long time ago.

Nursing taught me precision, high-stakes decision-making, and an obsessive attention to detail. It trained me to stay calm under pressure, follow complex protocols, and care deeply about outcomes. What it didn't teach me — I went out and learned myself. Through curiosity, late nights, a brilliant roommate, and a stubborn refusal to be boxed in, I picked up CAD modelling, electrical design, video production, and AI model evaluation, not as a career pivot, but because I genuinely love building things.

Today I sit at a rare intersection: the analytical rigour of healthcare meets the creative precision of engineering and digital media. I can evaluate an AI model's STEM output in the morning, render a 3D CAD assembly in the afternoon, and edit a polished video by evening. I thrive in remote environments, adapt quickly to new tools, and bring both clinical discipline and creative energy to everything I touch.

If you need someone who is genuinely multi-skilled — not just on paper — I am that person.
